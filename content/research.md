# Research


## Urban Economics

### The Location Curse:
FUA Integration and Industrial Hollowing in China


This project studies whether metropolitan integration
benefits surrounding regions.


Methods:

- Continuous Dose-response DID
- Instrumental Variables
- Spatial Regression Discontinuity


Main finding:

A 1 SD increase in FUA coverage reduces manufacturing output
by approximately 11%.
